🎯 Aim of the Experiment

1. To determine the rank of a given matrix using row reduction (echelon form) method.

2. Understand the relationship between rank, linear independence, and solutions of linear systems.

3. Visualize the effect of elementary row operations on the rank.

4. Apply rank concept to check consistency of non-homogeneous equations.

5. Develop computational thinking via interactive simulation.


After this lab, you will be able to compute matrix rank efficiently and interpret its significance in engineering mathematics.
