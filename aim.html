🎯 Aim of the Experiment

1. Understand the concept of Fourier series representation of periodic signals.

2. Visualize how adding sinusoidal harmonics approximates a given waveform.

3. Compute Fourier coefficients analytically for square, sawtooth, and triangle waves.

4. Observe the Gibbs phenomenon near discontinuities.

5. Apply Fourier series in engineering contexts (signal processing, vibrations).


By the end, you will be able to decompose periodic functions into Fourier components.
